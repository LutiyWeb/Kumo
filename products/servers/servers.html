Servers

KUMO servers are designed
to ease the digital transformation
of business
operations

An integrated approach to investing in the development of holistic IT infrastructure creates a "quantum leap" in technology development across the country.

This approach is more efficient and cost-effective than one-time investments in separate technologies. By investing in advanced technologies, we preserve and increase the profitability of investors. Maintaining old technologies is an irreplaceable loss of capital.

By creating new technologies and investing in modern solutions, we get a total reduction in the cost of ownership of these technologies, while obtaining an increase in return on investment (ROI).

Meet the challenges of the digital age

KUMO servers provide exceptional workload-optimized performance and hardware security features

KUMO servers are the most powerful and flexible data center operation solutions for companies of any size, regardless of industry and workload type.

These systems include expandable floor-standing servers for remote and branch offices, universal rack servers, compact and scalable blade systems, and horizontal scalable servers supporting high density components.

Whatever your computing challenges, you can solve them with KUMO solutions

These high quality systems are proven efficient, and the wide range of innovations allows businesses to reduce operating costs and complexity of infrastructure, expand the possibilities of everyday business operations and accelerate the process of obtaining a competitive advantage from implementing these IT solutions
