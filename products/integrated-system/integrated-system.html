Integrated

Systems

Building data center infrastructure is increasingly complex, time-consuming, and error-proof

The traditional approach, combining best-in-class components, is a complex and time-consuming process. And this "do it yourself" approach exposes companies to many risks.

There is a more efficient and cost-effective solution: KUMO integrated systems, an extensive and constantly expanding range of powerful systems that can be pre-configured, integrated and tested. They come as a single package that includes servers, storage systems, network switches and software, thus significantly reducing complexity.

The family of KUMO solutions

KUMO Solution 01 / 02

Integrated KUMO systems for ERP applications

KUMO Solution 02 / 02

Integrated KUMO, offering high-performance computing and big data analytics

With advanced management and administration capabilities, KUMO for ERP provides an optimized work environment for all ERP landscapes. Centralized ERP software components can be dynamically deployed physically or virtually, depending on business requirements.

The result is the faster implementation of innovations and, ultimately, greater opportunities for business and its adaptation to changing market conditions. It can be used with all types of IT service delivery, either locally, as a managed service or hosting service, or deployed in the cloud.

Technologies, such as usage scenarios and GPUs for deep learning, high-performance computing architectures and big-data analytics, increase the speed of Artificial Intelligence. Self-driving vehicles, personalized services, image analysis, preventive maintenance, or sensor data usage - all of these resource-intensive workloads require a fast, secure, reliable, and scalable infrastructure.

KUMO will provide reference architecture that brings customers a complete set of recommendations based on workloads and usage scenarios. This includes hardware and software as well as services that can be adapted to an existing HPC or large data infrastructure or created from scratch.
