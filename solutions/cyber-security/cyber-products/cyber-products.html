NoiseSocket

02

Perfect for IoT extensible service with simple wire protocol and minimal overhead.

NoiseSocket can encode Noise messages and associated negotiation data into a form suitable for transmission over reliable, stream-based protocols such as TCP.Stores and manages User Cards with Public Keys and associated information.

Small and simple codebase, works without certificates, build in padding and fast crypto primitives by default.

One of the patterns (Noise_IK, O-RTT) has formal security proofby WireGuard

Transport Secure Protocol encrypts any TCP connection

Advanced option like O-RTT, Perfect Forward Secrecy and pre-shared keys
