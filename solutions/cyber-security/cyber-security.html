Cyber

Security

Protect your business with security solutions from KUMO

Mobility and interaction are key in everyday business life. Managers travel a lot, exchanging valuable information every day via WLAN, UMTS and other available networks.

Therefore, the issue of data protection becomes more and more important. KUMO is the exclusive representatives of Virgil Security - a world leading cyber security company – on the Asian market. This partnership enables us to offer communities, governments and businesses the most cutting-edge security products.

Security Management Services

01

Professional security services

02
KUMO's professional security services provide access to the knowledge and skills needed to optimize security and maintain business continuity, as well as optimize costs. Our professional security services free up data center resources and allow you to focus on your core business objectives to gain competitive advantage. We offer independent consulting services to meet all security requirements.

Our services enable you to meet any security requirement with a wide range of professional services - all aspects of cloud assessment, information availability, IT risk assessment, security, business continuity and disaster recovery consulting services, data loss prevention assessment, identity management, training and awareness, technical design and technical support.

We tailor our professional services to the specific requirements of your business and help you resolve issues based on independent advice from our security specialists.
