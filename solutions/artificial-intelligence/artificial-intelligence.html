Artificial

Intelligence

Previous phases of digitalization have provided billions of people worldwide with broadband access, mobile communications and the Internet of Things, bringing a vast amount of data and information into people’s daily lives.

Today, connecting people, things and information in real time, we are entering an era of hyper-connectedness, where artificial intelligence can be applied in real everyday business situations.

The world will no longer be the same. In many cases — from handwriting recognition to mathematical exams — artificial intelligence has already proven to be more accurate than human specialists.

Artificial Intelligence and KUMO

People are central to the KUMO’s concepts, and Artificial Intelligence solutions are focused on providing citizens with new opportunities, increasing efficiency and supporting the work they do.

The impact of AI on our lives cannot be seen or appreciated in a single day. But one thing is certain: the impact is constantly and inevitably increasing.

KUMO integrates various artificial intelligence technologies and development flows. KUMO AI is neither a service nor a product. It is a common platform for the wide range of AI functions available to KUMO customers.

The KUMO AI provides a "people-centric" approachto collaboratively creating services and solutions.

It uses best-in-class global technologies designed and deployed to address consumers’ ever-growing challenges. By combining the strength of KUMO AI with carefully selected partner capabilities, we can offer optimal AI solutions that help overcome the challenges our clients face.

With KUMO AI solution, we are adding a wide range of value-added services to portfolio aimed at providing our customers with the ability to confidently carry out digitalization.
