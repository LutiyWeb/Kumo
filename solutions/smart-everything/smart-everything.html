Smart Utilities

01

The Internet of Things has the chance to make utilities and public services progressively more efficient by giving property holders, building supervisors, and city organizers more information around patterns and variances in utility use.

This information can have a positive effect, both monetarily and on natural asset utilization.

At the point when you hear terms like smart home, smart building, and smart city, it is the exceptionally fine degree of tracking and improvement of utility use that IoT adopters are hoping to accomplish.

Asset Tracking

02

Organizations moving enormous amounts of merchandise are adopting IoT by putting sensors on items or holders to monitor products shipping.

These trackers ensure item loss is negligible and can likewise precisely estimate how well products are selling or where they are moving quickly by utilizing smart gadgets organizations can

Retail, assembling, and farming are only three spheres that can use IoT item tracking to their advantage.

Smart Logistics

03

The Internet of Things gives insight that empowers the most exceptional logistics tracking systems .

Organizations can move goods quicker irregardless of ever-changing supply chains.

Smart City

04

The Smart City concept is a system that makes the best use of existing city resources and services and provides the greatest convenience to citizens.

This requires a close connection between Smart City projects (street surveillance, public services, intelligent transport system and others) on a metropolitan scale.

Smart City opportunities

01

Smart transport

An intelligent transport system is one of the most important components of a Smart City. It includes traffic lights, traffic monitoring detectors and CCTV cameras. The data is transferred to a data center, where it is analyzed in real time, which helps manage the situation on the roads. In the future, this information will enable city officials to make traffic forecasts when planning road blocks, the introduction of one-way traffic or opening a dedicated lane for buses and trolleybuses.

02

Electronic services

The online payment of various penalties and other services, transferred to the cloud, make it possible to pay fines and other duties without having to leave home

Internet doctor

This service helps one find the nearest hospital and make an online appointment to see a doctor. In a few years post implementation, the electronic registrar had been found to reduce queues in clinics by 2.5 times.

03

"Our City" and "Active Citizen"

Bringing the ability to interact directly with the city government and influence the life of the city, the "Our city" platform allows the city government to obtain feedback from citizens in real time. You can use it to complain about the work of officials and public services.

You can report the absence of a trash can in the park, damage to the stairs or littering, broken paving tiles, poor maintenance of trees and bushes or a pothole.

The “Active Citizen” platform of electronic referendums allows you to express your opinion on a variety of urban issues: from additional bus routes and mowing the lawn, to choosing a name for a new subway line.

04

E-School

The main elements of the E-School project are the electronic journal and day planner, and the library of electronic materials, which contains teaching aids and lesson scenarios. The scripts have replaced lesson plans and are similar to a presentation with visual materials and assignments. Teachers all over the city can take a suitable scenario from the library, complement an existing one or create a new one and share it.

This system allows sharing of experiences and creates healthy competition among teachers.
