5G Technology

5G is a transformational technology that provides increased speed, reduced latency and improved connectivity for smartphones everywhere.

Current smartphone usage can take advantage of the increased speed, while brand new computing games, XR and AI can be "on the go" based on experience. This makes it necessary to improve the performance of smartphones. KUMO helps prepare these 5G devices with solutions based on its high-performance and efficient architecture.

KUMO approach

Our societies and cities are faced with the challenges of improving security, energy efficiency, effectiveness of transportation and quality of living.

There is a growing need for a new generation service infrastructure that provides a digital ecosystem in smart cities, enabling the development of smart city infrastructure and services, thereby opening novel service and business opportunities for companies and new micro-operators

Current mobile networks will soon be insufficient to serve the increasing number of users and bandwidth-intensive digital services. This bottleneck threatens the realization of important smart city digital services, which require an effective and reliable telecommunications network for connection and distribution

KUMO has been investigating the technological opportunities and economic feasibility of the digital ecosystem, by developing proof-of-concept prototypes and demonstrating concrete technical solutions for 5G infrastructure.

The project will make big data capacity available and provide an open access platform for new digital services – both for technology and business.
