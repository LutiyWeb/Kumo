Application

Services

Our vision for application services is outlined in the following points:

We utilize advances technologies and approaches, while professionally applying and constantly developing new IoT, big data, machine learning, AI, image analysis, AR, DevOps/Continuous Delivery (CD) skills.

We know how to deal with complex enterprise IT landscapes that have evolved for decades. We’ve accumulated experience from multiple projects and learned the tricks to tame the various systems of various nature and architectural patterns.

We are flexible in terms of cooperation, ready to cooperate with third-party vendors and deliver business value to customers. If the customer’s needs and priorities change, we can adjust the project’s scope on the go and adapt service timelines to the new context.

We focus on TCO of the solutions we deliver and manage. We analyze how to optimize operational, maintenance, and other costs during a solution’s life cycle.
