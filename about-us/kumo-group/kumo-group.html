KUMO Group of companies is a centralized structure consisting of several individual enterprises established for the most efficent interactions. Each of the companies has a specific function within the structure.

KUMO Group structure

KUMO Capital Partners is an international investment fund headquartered in US.

The fund's area of interest includes both privately-owned and international companies operating in the field of IoT and with strong potential.
The objective of KUMO Capital is to integrate such companies
into the KUMO infrastructure.

Internally, the structure of the company can be divided into two components, represented by General Partners (GP) and Limited Partners (LP).

by General Partners (GP) and Limited Partners (LP).

GP – Managing company

GP are formulating decisions regarding projects of financing and transactions.

LP – Investors

LP can nominate one authorized person to the board of directors on their behalf, thus ensuring investor control over the allocation of funds and decision-making process.
